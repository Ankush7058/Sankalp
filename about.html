Creating Superlative Communities With Trust

Our company was started 20 years back by our founder – Mr. Uttam Appa Shinde in Nashik, Igatpuri. Under his purview, the company sold over 200 acres of land in Nashik which is a milestone in itself.

As an organization, Sankalp believes not just in selling our lands and villas, but we also endeavor to welcome new members to our family and give them healthy returns.

In the future, we want to give our customers multiple options to choose from depending on their budget. We’ll be offering studio apartments and budget friendly homes in the lap of nature.

Our Guiding Principles

Vision

To develop a reputation of being India's best developers by creating properties that bring people closer to nature while ensuring excellent returns on investment.

Mission

To make healthier communities by connecting people to nature through thoughtfully designed spaces that promote well-being and sustainable living.

Philosophy

To create blossoming communities with trust, prosperity and commitment, where every transaction is a beginning of a long-term relationship.
